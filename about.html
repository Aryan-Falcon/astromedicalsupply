About Us

Home medical equipment, with a neighborhood feel

Astro Medical Supply & Equipment is a Houston-based supplier of durable medical equipment (DME) and home care supplies, based on Main St in the Texas Medical Center district.

Our Approach

Equipment for the home, sourced with care

Coming home from the hospital, managing a chronic condition, or caring for a family member all come with a list of equipment and supplies to figure out. Astro Medical Supply & Equipment exists to make that list simpler — one place to find respiratory, wound care, mobility, and daily living supplies, with staff on hand to help you choose the right item.

We're registered as a durable medical equipment supplier and located minutes from the Texas Medical Center, one of the largest medical complexes in the world — which puts us close to the hospitals, clinics, and patients we serve every day.

Comfort first

Our name comes from our mission: helping patients recover and manage care comfortably at home.

Straightforward service

Call, fax a prescription, or stop by — we keep the ordering process simple.

Broad catalog

From oxygen concentrators to compression stockings, most home care needs are covered in one visit.

Community location

Based in the 77030 medical corridor, alongside the hospitals and clinics of the TMC.
