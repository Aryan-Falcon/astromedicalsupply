Insurance & Billing

Common questions

Do you accept insurance?

We work with a range of insurance plans and can help verify your benefits. Call our office with your insurance information for specifics on your plan.

Do I need a prescription?

Most durable medical equipment and supplies require a prescription from your doctor. You can fax it to us at (832) 924-4010 ahead of your visit.

Do you deliver?

Ask our staff about delivery options for your area when you call — availability can depend on the item and your location.

Can I just walk in?

Yes — visit us at 7700 Main St, Ste 210, Houston, TX 77030 during business hours. Calling ahead helps us confirm an item is in stock.
